— OUR LEGACY

Serving Nizamabad
Since 1949

Founded in the year India found its independence, Dawood Watch Company has been a quiet keeper of time for three generations. From the bustling lanes of Nehru Park to the homes of Nizamabad, our story is woven into the heartbeat of this city.

For over seven decades we have curated wrist watches, wall clocks, table pieces and Islamic prayer-time watches from the world's most respected names — combining craftsmanship, heritage and trust in every tick.

One family. One showroom. A lifetime devoted to time.
